Donation

We appreciate your generosity!

As a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, we rely on the generous donations made by those who support our work. Please help us enhance the quality of life of cancer patients in our local community by making a donation through our secure PayPal donation link below.

If you would like to make a donation in memory of or in honor of an individual, please provide the following information in the PayPal box designated as “add special instructions for the recipient” – name of honoree, name and address of person to whom gift should be acknowledged, and the name and address of individual(s) making the donation.

 

Donations via check should be made payable to “Thomas & McMahan Cancer Foundation” and mailed to:
